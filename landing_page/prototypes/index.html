Design prototypes

Five takes on a new jesselumarie.com. All of them work on a phone — tap one to try it.

Retro RPG
Final Fantasy VII Menu
The site as a save-file menu: gradient blue windows, HP/MP bars, a limit gauge that charges, gil that accrues while you idle, and arrow-key navigation with the classic cursor blip.
 Warm Minimal
Modern & Simple
A quiet, editorial-serif personal site. Warm paper tones, big Fraunces headline, link cards, a selected-writing list, and automatic dark mode.
 CLI
Terminal
jesse@boulder:~$ — a working shell. It boots by typing "whoami" and "ls", and you can run commands like help, history, open writing, and sudo (don't).
 Brutalist
Editorial Poster
Big type, thick black rules, a scrolling ticker, and a numbered index that inverts on hover. Feels like a broadsheet front page.
 Bento Grid
Bento
A playful grid of tiles — intro card with your photo, live local time, recent writing, and a gradient "say hello" button. Reflows to two columns on phones.
